* Dotfiles
** Available Configs
   - emacs (and folder)
   - vim
   - tmux
   - bashrc
   - mpd
   - ncmpcpp
   - polybar

** Locations of Configs
*** mpd
    ~/.mpd/
*** ncmpcpp
    ~/.ncmpcpp/
*** polybar
    ~/.config/polybar/
*** vim
    ~/.vim/
*** tmux
    ~/
*** emcas config
    ~/
*** emcas directory
    ~/.emacs.d/
